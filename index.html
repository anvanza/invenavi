Hardware

  • PI Camera (normal version)

  • Standard Size - High Torque - Metal Gear Servo
  • Mtroniks Viper Marine 20
  • Aluminum Double Rudder
  • 2.5:1 gearbox motor, 4.5-15V 540 motor
  • Pursuit V-Hull Boat
